# Item Detail Screen: Redesign Mockups

## Current Problems

1. **Photos are completely missing** вҖ” items have photos but the detail screen only shows the emoji
2. **No visual hierarchy** вҖ” Cost, Value, ROI, Asking, Comps, eBay, Community, Marketplaces all compete equally
3. **Decision-making info is buried** вҖ” "Should I sell? At what price?" isn't answerable at a glance
4. **Action buttons at the very bottom** вҖ” Edit, Research, Search Market are after 5+ scrollable sections
5. **Comps section is overwhelming** вҖ” CRUD form + eBay + Community + Marketplaces = information overload
6. **No profit/loss clarity** вҖ” ROI is just a percentage tucked into a 3-column grid

7. **"Mark as Sold"** вҖ” prominent CTA for the most important action
8. **Delete is clearly separated** вҖ” at the very bottom, less accidental

---

## My Recommendation: Option C

The current screen treats every piece of info equally. Option C creates a clear **decision funnel**:

1. **See it** (photos)
2. **Know your position** (profit/loss)
3. **Take action** (search/research/sell)
4. **Go deeper if needed** (comps, details)

This mirrors how traders think: "What do I have? Am I up or down? What should I do?"
